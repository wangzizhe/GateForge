## 2) Content Authorization Rules

Without prior written permission, the following are explicitly prohibited:

- Reproduction or republication
- Adaptation, translation, or derivative works
- Commercial use
- Any AI/ML-related use, including but not limited to:
  - Model training
  - Fine-tuning
  - Evaluation or benchmarking
  - Dataset construction
  - Embedding/vectorization
  - Retrieval-augmented generation corpora
  - Any automated ingestion pipeline for machine learning systems

## 3) AI Training Restriction Notice

Without prior written permission, no content on this site may be used for AI model training, fine-tuning, evaluation, or dataset construction.
